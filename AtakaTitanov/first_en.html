Attack on Titan: Season 1

Rating: 8.8 / 10 Year: 2013 Director: Tetsurō Araki Studio: Wit Studio

Season 1 Description


The world of humans is on the brink of extinction due to the appearance of giant creatures — Titans, who devour people. To survive, the remnants of humanity hide behind huge walls. But one day, a Colossal Titan breaks the outer wall, and a horde of Titans breaks through. Eren Yeager, who witnesses his mother's death, vows to destroy all Titans. Together with Mikasa and Armin, he joins the Scout Regiment to take revenge and uncover the truth about Titans.
