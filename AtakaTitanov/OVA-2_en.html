Attack on Titan: No Regrets

Rating: 8.5 / 10 Year: 2014 Director: Tetsurou Araki Studio: Wit Studio

Description of Attack on Titan: No Regrets


This OVA shows the past of Levi and Eren, and how they became who we know them to be. Levi decides that to survive, he must become part of a cruel and ruthless fight. Eren, on his path, faces moral dilemmas.
