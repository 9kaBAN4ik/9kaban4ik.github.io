Description of Attack on Titan OVA-1


The first "Attack on Titan" OVA reveals additional details about the lives of the main characters. They face an unknown threat beyond the walls, making them reflect on their fears, dreams, and the power of friendship. This episode gives a deeper look into the inner world of Eren, Mikasa, and Armin.
