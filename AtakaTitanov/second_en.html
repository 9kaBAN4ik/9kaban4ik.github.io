Attack on Titan: Season 2

Rating: 8.4 / 10 Year: 2017 Director: Masashi Koizumi Studio: Wit Studio

Description of Season 2


After the devastating events of the first season, the heroes face a new threat: Titans have breached the inner walls. Eren, Mikasa, and Armin, along with the Scout Regiment, try to uncover the origins of the Titan threat. They will encounter many unexpected discoveries, including the revealing of the hidden identities of the Colossal and Armored Titans. Season 2 unveils many secrets about the world beyond the walls and brings us closer to the answer to the main question — where do the Titans come from?
