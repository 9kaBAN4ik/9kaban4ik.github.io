Red Dead Redemption 2

Rating: 9.8 / 10 Year: 2018 Developer: Rockstar Games

Game Description: Red Dead Redemption 2

"Red Dead Redemption 2" is an open-world action-adventure game set in the American Wild West. You play as Arthur Morgan, a member of the Van der Linde gang, as you navigate through the challenges of outlaw life. The game features a vast open world, a compelling story, and a deep sense of immersion, with rich character development and moral dilemmas.
