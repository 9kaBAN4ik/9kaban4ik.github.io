Game Description: Hogwarts Legacy

"Hogwarts Legacy" is an action role-playing game set in the Harry Potter universe. You play as a student at Hogwarts in the 1800s, mastering magic, solving mysteries, and exploring a rich open world. The game offers deep RPG mechanics, character customization, and a unique story where your decisions shape the magical world around you.
